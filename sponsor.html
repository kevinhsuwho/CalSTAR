SPONSORS
CalSTAR wishes to thank its sponsors. Your monetary and material contributions promote the growth of students as engineers and proclaim a commitment to education and the future. To find more information on being a sponsor, take a look at our sponsorship packet below, or please email us at ucberkeleystar@gmail.com.
The Associated Students of the University of California are the elected representatives of the UC Berkeley student body. As the collective voice of the students, they build student power through leadership development, and through grassroots campaigns and coalitions.
CalSTAR announced its partnership with Northrop Grumman starting Spring 2016. Northrop Grumman has been supporting us financially and we thank them for helping us launch our team in the right direction.
CalSTAR thanks SolidWorks for providing us with copies of CAD software for the team. This will allow us to CAD our rocket for testing, compatability and collaboration.
CalSTAR thanks Morrison Foerster for supporting us financially and we thank them for helping us get our team off the ground.
CalSTAR thanks Boeing for supporting us financially and helping us fund new projects and future research.
CalSTAR thanks Bay Area Circuits for sponsoring us with electronics equipment necessary to control our projects.
